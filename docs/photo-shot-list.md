## Technical Requirements

- **Format:** JPG or WebP, minimum 2000px wide
- **Hero images:** Aim for 2400x1350px (16:9) or wider
- **OG image:** Need one master crop at exactly 1200x630px
- **Place photos:** At least 1200x800px
- **File naming:** Use descriptive names like `znjan-promenade-sunset.jpg`, `taboo-beach-club-exterior.jpg`
- **Orientation:** Landscape preferred for heroes and cards; portrait OK for detail/texture shots
- **Lighting:** Golden hour (6-8am, 5-7pm summer) gives best results. Avoid harsh midday sun.
- **Weather:** Blue sky days preferred. Dramatic clouds work for atmospheric shots.

## Image Processing Pipeline

After taking photos:
1. Select best shots per category
2. Basic editing (exposure, white balance, crop)
3. Export at 2400px wide as JPG (quality 85%)
4. Place in `src/assets/images/` organized by folder:
   - `src/assets/images/hero/` — hero images
   - `src/assets/images/places/` — venue photos
   - `src/assets/images/beach-areas/` — zone photos
   - `src/assets/images/activities/` — activity shots
   - `src/assets/images/infrastructure/` — practical shots
5. Astro's image pipeline will auto-generate WebP/AVIF and responsive sizes
