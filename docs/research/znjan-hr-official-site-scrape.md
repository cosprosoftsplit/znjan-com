## 3. ZNJANSKI PLATO DEVELOPMENT PROJECT

### Project Title
"ITU -- Uredenje Znjanskog platoa" (Znjan Plateau Development)
Co-financed by the European Regional Development Fund (ERDF) under the Integrated Territorial Programme 2021-2027.

### Key Figures

|--------|-------|
| **Total project value** | EUR 45,768,900.70 |
| **Eligible costs** | EUR 17,760,530.79 |
| **EU funding awarded** | EUR 13,940,591.01 (78.49% of eligible costs) |
| **Construction contract value** | EUR 45.5 million (excl. VAT) |
| **Supervision contract** | EUR 183,980 (excl. VAT) |

### Concession Area Breakdown
| Section | Area |
|---------|------|
| **Total** | 218,322 m2 |
| **Marine section** | 95,176 m2 |
| **Land section** | 123,146 m2 |

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|

| Detail | Value |

| **Phase 2 (garage)** | 365 days |
| **Phase 6** | Conducted separately (hotel area) |

### Planned Facilities (from official site)
- Approximately 48,000 m2 of green space (second-largest in Split)
- Promenade walkways
- Bicycle paths
- Equipped beaches with splash areas
- Children's playground
- Sports facilities
- Amphitheater for cultural/musical events (with steel dome/cupola)
- Hospitality and tourist services
- Multiple public recreation areas
- Underground parking garage: 530 spaces
- Open-air parking: 451 spaces

### Vegetation Plan (official data)
- 730 new trees and palms to be planted
- 12,815 native shrubs and perennials
- Analysis revealed 2/3 of existing trees could be saved through relocation or retention
- Design enables year-round usage with summer shade and winter sunlight
- Full landscaping potential expected within 10 years

## 7. ZAPADNA OBALA (WESTERN WATERFRONT)

### Key Facts
- Opened: April 2013
- Total developed surface: 11,700 m2
- Extended in 2015 past former Hotel Ambasador
- Stretches from southern entrance of Diocletian's Cellars to Baluni Bay

### Features
- Native Dalmatian vegetation
- Bicycle pathways
- Modern swimming pool and two fountains
- Contemporary urban furniture (teak benches and lounge chairs)
- Three hospitality venues

### Administrative History
- **2022:** 2-meter coastal strip (1,452.76 m2) removed from commercial management, transferred to Port Authority
- **2023:** Croatian Government expanded port boundaries; Western Waterfront officially designated as part of Port of Split
- **2024:** Split City Council merged Splitska obala d.o.o. into Znjan d.o.o., transferring waterfront concession management

## 9. EVENTS & MANIFESTATIONS

Znjan d.o.o. organizes events across Split, not just at Znjan beach. Key events:

### Advent u Splitu (Advent in Split)
- Locations: Riva, Prokurativas, Vocni trg (Fruit Square), Pjaca (main square), Sperun, Dardin park, Stobrec
- Features "splitske kucice" (small Split houses/stalls)
- Concerts, DJ performances, Christmas fairy tale activities for children
- 2024/2025 season included ice skating rinks at Prokurativas and Mertojak park
- Co-organized with Split Tourism Board

### Docek Nove Godine (New Year's Eve on Riva)
- Over 50,000 visitors at 2024/2025 celebration
- Featured: Dubioza Kolektiv, DJ Antoine, TBF, Psihomodo Pop (various years)
- Daytime program starts at 11 AM
- Fireworks at midnight

### Sudamja (St. Duje/Dometius Day -- Split City Day)
- City's patron saint celebration
- 2024: "Never longer, never richer, never more diverse"
- Over 30 locations throughout the city
- 100+ events (religious, cultural, entertainment, sports)
- Performers included Petar Graso, 4 Tenora, Grse, Vojko V, Natali Dizdar, Matija Cvek
- Street processions with tens of thousands of residents

### Splitski Krnjeval (Split Carnival)
- 10-day carnival celebration (late February -- early March)
- Masks receive keys to the city
- Culminates in Maja Suput concert (recent years)
- Merry masked parade through city streets

### Praznik Cvijeca (Flower Festival)
- 47th edition held in 2024 (theme: "Marulicev cvit -- Split")
- March dates across Riva, Vocni trg, and Diocletian's Palace basements
- ~50 florists from Croatia, Poland, Hungary, Italy, Slovenia, China
- Main floral designer: Simon Ogrizek
- Organized with Udruzenje obrtnika Split (Split Craftsmen's Association)

### Domestic Split
- Traditional crafts and domestic products fair
- 5-day event during June on the Riva
- Timed with cruise ship arrivals
- Family farm products, healthy food, handicrafts, artistic creations
- Live klapa singing, folk ensembles, craft workshops

### Spalatum Expo
- Tourism destination festival at start of tourist season
- 4 days on Split's waterfront
- Sections: souvenir exhibition + tourism offerings
- Categories: travel agencies, health tourism, sports clubs, hotels, rural farms, cultural events

### HANDS Festival
- Intangible heritage festival
- Held in Diocletian's Palace basements
- 5-day event
- Traditional crafts and heritage celebration

### Vridne Ruke (Skilled Hands) -- Fair
- Craft fair timed with Dan Pobjede (Victory Day, August 5)

### Dan Splitskog Sporta (Split Sports Day)
- Celebration of athletes and sports clubs

### Notable One-Time Events
- Docek vaterpolista (Water polo team reception -- world champions)
- Docek olimpijaca na Rivi (Olympic athletes reception from Paris)
- Dalmatino concert on Riva for Dan Pobjede (Victory Day)
