## 2. Žnjanski Plato — Project Facts

### Scale
- **Total concession area:** 218,322 m²
  - Sea: 95,176 m²
  - Land: 123,146 m²
- **Green space:** ~48,000 m² (Split's second-largest park)
- **Vegetation:** ~730 new trees and palms + 12,815 native shrubs and perennials

### Financials
- **Total project value:** €45.77 million
- **Construction cost:** €45.5 million (without VAT)
- **Supervision cost:** €183,980 (without VAT)
- **EU funding:** €13.94 million (78.49% of eligible costs of €17.76 million)
- **Eligible costs:** €17.76 million
- **Annual lease revenue:** €2.8 million (from 11 pavilions)
- **Programme:** ITU – European Regional Development Fund, Integrated Territorial Program 2021-2027

### Timeline
- **Project origins:** 1998
- **Architectural competition:** 2017
- **Winning architect:** Ante Kuzmanić (selected February 2018)
- **Concession signed:** November 2022
- **Building permits finalized:** January 2024
- **Construction started:** February 2, 2024 (ceremonially opened by Mayor Ivica Puljak)
- **Main contractor:** Lavčević d.d. + Lavčević-inženjering d.o.o. (Split)
- **Supervision:** Geoprojekt d.d. + Sedra Consulting d.o.o.
- **Building permits issued:** All finalized
- **Concrete work completed:** Late 2024
- **Amphitheatre dome completed:** Early 2025
- **Horticultural finishing:** March 2025
- **Grand opening:** Saturday, June 21, 2025 (first day of summer)
- **Parking charges began:** Wednesday, July 16, 2025
- **Lease agreements signed:** January 30, 2025 (from December 2024 public auction)

### Quote
> "Pored našeg Žnjana, ča će nam Copacabana" — ("Beside our Žnjan, who needs Copacabana")

### Grand Opening (June 21, 2025)
- **Attendees:** Mayor Tomislav Šuta, Minister Tonči Glavina, Parliament members, city/county officials
- **Mayor Šuta quote:** "We have gained a location that represents more than just a beach. It's a gathering place for citizens to enjoy various events, cultural activities, and sports programs vital to Split."
- **Minister Glavina quote:** "Such spaces transform how cities appear, enhance quality of living, and reshape how citizens experience their area."
- **Entertainment:** TBF, Ante Gelo with orchestra, Zorica Kondža, Alen Nižetić, Albina, Đorđe Peruzović, Goran Karan, Tajči
- **Features:** Contemporary dance, classical ballet, acrobatics, drone displays, fireworks

## 3. Facilities

### Parking
- **Underground garage:** 530 spaces (2 levels, construction: 365 days)
- **Outdoor parking:** 451 spaces
- **Total:** ~981 parking spaces
- **Charges began:** July 16, 2025
- **Hourly rate:** €1 (confirmed from official press release)
- **Pricing:** Full "Cjenik parking 2026" PDF on znjan.hr
- **Notice to residents:** Separate notice issued for Žnjan area residents

### Beach Infrastructure
- Organized beaches with splash pads
- Beach for people with disabilities (opened summer 2024, before main opening)
  - Hydraulic elevator for wheelchair users
  - Adapted WC and changing cabin
  - Shower facilities
  - Umbrellas
  - Lifeguards and assistants
  - Floating safety barriers
- Safety railings, water entry stairs
- Benches, waste bins, showers, changing rooms across all beaches

### Sports & Recreation
- Children's playground (~600 m²)
- Skate park (~1,200 m²)
- Sports playgrounds (beach volleyball courts, etc.)
- Walking paths
- Cycling trails
- Aquapark (public tender issued — operator TBD)
- Diving club (public tender issued)
- Windsurfing club (public tender issued)
- Tourist medical clinic — 36.57 m², €4,000/year, must operate Jun 1–Sep 30, 8AM–8PM

### Cultural
- **Amphitheatre** — with steel dome (completed early 2025)
- Named squares: Trg kulture, Vodeni trg, Topografski trg, Trg 14 palmi

### Commercial
- **11 pavilion buildings** (leased via FINA Licitator anonymous auction, December 2024)
- Tender categories:
  - 3 restaurants (designations: A-S-PP1, B-S1, B-S2)
  - 6 bistro/café bars (A-U1, A-U2, B-U1, B-U2, C-U3, PL-S)
  - 1 mini market (C-U2-PP2)
  - 1 kiosk/trafika (A-S-PP2)
- **Lease terms:** 10-year contracts
- **Tourist ambulance** — separate tender

## 4. Zapadna Obala (Western Waterfront)

Managed by Žnjan d.o.o. since 2024 merger.

- **Area:** 11,700 m² of developed public space
- **Location:** Southern entrance of Diocletian's Palace cellars to Baluni Cove
- **Features:** Modern swimming pool with 2 fountains, bicycle lane, teak benches & loungers, 3 hospitality establishments, Mediterranean vegetation
- **Opened:** 2013 (initial), extended 2015
