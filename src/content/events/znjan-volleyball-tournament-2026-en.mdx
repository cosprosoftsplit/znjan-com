## Znjan Beach Volleyball Tournament 2026

Get ready for a weekend of sun, sand, and competitive spirit at the annual Znjan Beach Volleyball Tournament. Taking place across two action-packed days at the Znjan Sports Zone, this tournament is open to **amateur teams of all skill levels**.

Teams of four can register in advance through our website. The tournament follows a group-stage format on Saturday, with knockout rounds and finals on Sunday. **Prizes will be awarded to the top three teams**, including trophies and vouchers from local sponsors.

Whether you are a seasoned player or just getting started, this is your chance to compete in a friendly yet thrilling atmosphere right on the Adriatic shore. Spectators are welcome free of charge — bring a towel, grab a cold drink from the nearby beach bars, and cheer on your favourite teams.

Registration closes on **July 5, 2026**. Spaces are limited, so sign up early to secure your spot.
