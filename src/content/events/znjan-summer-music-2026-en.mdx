## Znjan Summer Music Festival 2026

Experience three extraordinary nights of music under the stars at the Znjan Summer Music Festival. Set in the stunning **Znjan Beach Amphitheatre** overlooking the Adriatic, this festival brings together an exciting lineup of Croatian favourites and international artists for the ultimate end-of-summer celebration.

The festival features **multiple stages** spread across the beach area. The Main Stage hosts headline acts each evening, while the Chill Zone offers acoustic sets and DJ performances for those who prefer a more relaxed vibe. A dedicated **Discovery Stage** showcases up-and-coming talent from across the region.

Beyond the music, the festival boasts an impressive **food court area** with over twenty vendors serving cuisines from across the Mediterranean and beyond. From wood-fired pizzas and fresh seafood platters to artisan desserts, there is something for every palate.

**Tickets are available in single-day and full-festival passes.** Early-bird pricing is available until July 15, 2026. VIP packages include reserved seating, backstage access, and complimentary refreshments. Whether you come for one evening or all three, the Znjan Summer Music Festival promises to be the highlight of your summer.
