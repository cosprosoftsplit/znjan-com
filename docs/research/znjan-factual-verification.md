### Construction Phases

The project was executed in **6 phases**:
- Phases 1, 3, 4, 5, 6 -- deadline: 289 days
- **Phase 2** (underground garage with 530 spaces) -- deadline: 365 days due to complexity
- Phase 6 was carried out separately as it covers land under concession of Split Hotel

### What Was Specifically Built (Verified)

- **11 pavilion buildings** for hospitality, retail, and recreation
- **Open-air amphitheater** with rooftop viewpoint
- **Underground garage** -- 530 parking spaces
- **451 outdoor parking spaces** (in addition to the garage)
- **~48,000 m2 of greenery** -- second-largest green space in Split after Marjan
- **730 trees and palm trees** planted
- **12,815 native shrubs and perennials** planted
- Promenades and bicycle paths along the coast
- **Children's playground** -- approximately 600 m2
- **Skate park** -- approximately 1,200 m2
- Sports courts: basketball, cage football, beach volleyball
- **Splash pad** and climbing frames
- Open multifunctional pavilion and open-air stage
- Named public squares: **Trg kulture** (Culture Square, ~460 m2), **Vodeni trg** (Water Square), **Topografski trg** (Topographic Square), **Trg 14 palmi** (14 Palms Square)
- Public restrooms, showers, changing rooms
- Diving platforms (skakaonice)
- Pontoon for public transport vessels

Sources: [znjan.hr/en/znjan-plato](https://znjan.hr/en/znjan-plato/), [Baustela.hr](https://baustela.hr/gradiliste/baustelska-reportaza-transformacija-platoa-znjan-najvece-gradiliste-uz-more/), [LiveCamCroatia](https://www.livecamcroatia.com/en/news/the-project-reconstruction-of-znjan/)

## 4. CURRENT STATE OF THE BEACH (as of February 2026)

### Grand Opening -- CONFIRMED

- **Date:** June 21, 2025 (summer solstice, first day of summer)
- **Ceremony name:** "Ca ce mi Copacabana"
- **Attendance:** Approximately 10,000 people
- **Hosts:** Iva Sulentic and Tarik Filipovic
- **Main concert:** Ante Gelo with 17 guest performers, held in the new amphitheater
- **Additional:** DJ Antoine performed and filmed a music video for his cover of Oliver Dragojevic's "Ca ce mi Copacabana" at Znjan

Sources: [Visit Split](https://visitsplit.com/en/7259/grand-opening-of-the-znjan-plateau), [split.hr](https://split.hr/kalendar/en/city-split-calendar/grand-opening-of-znjan-2025-ca-ce-mi-copacabana), [Croatia Week](https://www.croatiaweek.com/photos-znjan-officially-opens-in-split/)

### Completion Status -- MOSTLY COMPLETE, SOME FINISHING ONGOING

Multiple sources from summer 2025 indicate:
- The beach, promenades, amphitheater, greenery, sports facilities, and public infrastructure **opened on June 21, 2025**
- **Interior finishing of some pavilion/hospitality buildings** was still ongoing at the time of opening, expected to take "an additional 2-3 months"
- TripAdvisor reviews from late 2025 and into 2026 describe mixed experiences: some areas fully operational ("Everything is fully working, lots of space, great bars, places to eat and relax, clean sea"), while other reviewers noted "maps showing several planned restaurants and bars that have not finished yet"
- One reviewer flagged pricing: "50 euros for 2 sunbeds and an umbrella" was considered excessive

### Events Already Held at the New Znjan Plateau

From znjan.hr, these events have been organized since the opening:

- **Summer at Znjan** -- described as a program "for all tastes and generations"
- **Advent in Split / Christmas Fair** (Christmas season 2025)
- **New Year's Eve celebration** (December 31, 2025) -- with DJ Antoine performing world megahits
- **HANDS -- Festival of Intangible Heritage Split** (biannual: spring and autumn)
